HR Consulting

Our HR consulting services help organizations to focus on internal resources working towards their best and simultaneously helps them to control the bottom lines. It also helps the organization to gain cost reduction and time efficiencies and provide them with a cutting edge over their HR Strategies and improves service to their employees.

HR Consultants are responsible for assisting clients with strategically integrating effective HR processes, programs and practices into their daily operations and to maximize the client's performance related to human resources by introducing or marketing "best practice" products or services as well as to provide periodic feedback to clients regarding their performance related to annual management objectives. To accomplish this, we may need to perform needs assessments or audits and make recommendations or proposals, coordinate the creation and implementation of an action or corrective plan, and when required, organize and coordinate cross- functional Human Resource teams to assist the client with developing and implementing performance improvement corrective plans, programs or processes.

HR Solutions

HR Solution methodology, “Process Consulting” is a powerful process which is used to enhance effectiveness, improve and review current processes, and address conflicts. It helps teams to work together more effectively. The key to success is to blend both the practices that work within the company culture and the realities of business. It is rather a continues process with provision for control and review. HR Process consulting activity will be different for a small and entrepreneurial organization as compared to other established organizations.

Based on the organization needs Process Consulting services can be obtained in three ways.

  • Primary Consulting Services
  • Expert Consulting Services
  • Partner Consulting Services

HR Outsourcing

Company is going through the growing pains of a successful business. The organization may need help in HR, but don’t necessarily need a full HR Department. The Company may had a recent bad experience with an HR- related role in the company that could have been avoided if the Company had a more competent HR resource. HR Outsourcing would be the answer to the challenges with day-to-day activities. Whether the Company undergoes the following.

  • Statutory & Compliance issues
  • Payroll Management
  • Management support
  • Recruitment
  • On-site Support activities
  • Assistance in handling complicated employee issues

Training

The nature of the training is industry-specific, with the level of responsibility and variety of activities dependent on the type and size of organization, activities include, Identifying training and development needs, designing and expanding training and development programmers, training budgets, training calendar, considering the costs of planned programs and keeping within budgets as assessing the return on Investment.

Managing the delivery of training and development programs and, in a more senior role, devising a training strategy for the organization, monitoring and reviewing the progress of trainees, ensuring that statutory training requirements are met.
